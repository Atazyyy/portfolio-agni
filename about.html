My Journey

Background

My journey into AI began with a simple question: "How can I make this repetitive task faster and more reliable?" This led me to explore prompt engineering, API integrations, and automation pipelines.

I built PromptCraft — an interactive learning platform for mastering prompt engineering, featuring 12 lessons, 60+ quizzes, and a prompt playground. This project taught me the nuances of designing prompts that produce consistent, high-quality outputs.

With AtapDigital AI Prompt Library, I developed a practical tool that generates social media content using Claude API, demonstrating real-world workflow automation.

I'm drawn to the AI Specialist role at Nakama Creative Lab because it aligns perfectly with my interest in research automation, internal tools development, and helping teams work smarter with AI. I thrive in environments where I can apply computational thinking to solve non-technical problems and make AI a client-facing value.
